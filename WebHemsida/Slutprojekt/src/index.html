Welcome to SafePass!

Welcome to the website that helps you to make your accounts safer. Here you can find tips on how you as a user, can easily create and have safe accounts online against hackers and people that want to compromise your accounts.

If you just want to create a secure password you can use the password generator below. But if you want to know how to make your account overall secure you can visit this page or click the Info link in the menu!
